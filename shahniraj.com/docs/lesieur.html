Lesieur Cristal Corporate Site

A corporate web experience crafted to refresh Lesieur Cristal’s brand presence and improve stakeholder engagement across Morocco and global markets.

Client Orpheon Digital
Role UI/UX Design & WordPress Engineering
Year 2024
Stack WordPress, PHP, GSAP, HTML5, CSS3

Project Overview

I partnered with Orpheon Digital to reimagine Lesieur Cristal’s corporate website. The initiative combined a contemporary brand expression with a performant WordPress implementation, ensuring marketing teams could launch new campaigns and content without engineering support.

Responsibilities

  • Led experience design workshops to translate brand strategy into an accessible, responsive interface.
  • Engineered a custom WordPress theme with modular Gutenberg blocks to empower non-technical editors.
  • Integrated GSAP-driven micro-interactions to elevate perceived quality while maintaining performance budgets.
  • Optimised Core Web Vitals, accessibility, and SEO metadata to meet enterprise-grade requirements.

Impact

  • Reduced marketing team dependency on development by introducing reusable, brand-aligned content blocks.
  • Improved engagement metrics with clearer navigation, refreshed product storytelling, and multilingual support.
  • Delivered a scalable codebase with documentation for internal IT teams to maintain long term.
