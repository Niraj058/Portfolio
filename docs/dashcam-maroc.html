Dashcam-Maroc Commerce

A bespoke WooCommerce build that powers Morocco’s leading dashcam retailer—complete with automation, conversational commerce, and merchandising tooling tailored to a growing catalogue.

Client Dashcam-Maroc
Role WooCommerce & Automation Developer
Year 2022
Stack WooCommerce, WordPress, PHP, JavaScript

Project Overview

Dashcam-Maroc needed an ecommerce platform that could scale inventory, educate buyers, and reduce the manual workload on their lean operations team. I customized WooCommerce to deliver a frictionless buyer journey and connected backend systems that automated fulfilment touch points.

Responsibilities

  • Developed a custom WooCommerce theme supporting multi-language product storytelling and comparison tables.
  • Implemented WhatsApp-based notifications, CRM synchronisation, and invoicing using bespoke plugins and low-code automations.
  • Optimised checkout, shipping rules, and analytics to surface conversion insights in Google Analytics and Meta Ads.
  • Documented maintenance workflows so the in-house team could update accessories, pricing, and promotions confidently.

Impact

  • Enabled the team to launch new bundles in minutes instead of hours thanks to reusable WooCommerce presets.
  • Increased repeat purchases via automation that nudged customers about firmware updates and accessory compatibilities.
  • Improved customer response time by centralising enquiries from web forms, WhatsApp, and Facebook Messenger.
